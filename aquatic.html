Great Barrier Reef

Corals are tiny animals, when gathered they form a coral reef. The world's biggest coral reef is the Great barrier reef in Australia. There is a wide variety of life and species, among them are 1500 species of fish. The great barrier reef is over 2600 kilometers long and can be seen from space!
